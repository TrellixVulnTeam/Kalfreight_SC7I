Our History

Founded in 2014, Kal Freight Inc. is a large and continually-growing trucking company in California with over 700 trucks and 2,000 trailers and has over 800 employees. As a dynamic modern transportation company with a strategic corporate location based in Ontario, California, Kal Freight extended its reach to distribution warehouses located in Texas, Indiana, North Carolina, and New Jersey. In 2018, Kal Freight was named one of the best carriers for Uber Freight and by many renowned customers.

Kal Freight have owned and leased thousands of sq. ft. of modern warehouse space in above mentioned states which further helps in rendering services and executing the business professionally.
